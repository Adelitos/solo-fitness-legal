Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Solo Fitness ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal fitness tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Solo Fitness, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Health and fitness data: Workouts, exercise logs, meals, body measurements, sleep data, heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), recovery scores, stress levels, and other biometric data.
  • Device integrations: If you connect third-party services (such as Apple Health, Health Connect, or WHOOP), we receive health and fitness data you authorize from those services.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized fitness tracking, insights, and progress reports within the app.
  • Sync data between your connected devices and services.
  • Display your fitness metrics, trends, and history.
  • Enable social features you opt into (leaderboards, activity feed).

3. Third-Party Integrations

Solo Fitness integrates with the following services at your discretion:

  • Apple Health / Health Connect: We read and write health data with your explicit permission. You can revoke access at any time through your device settings or the app.
  • WHOOP: If you connect your WHOOP account, we receive recovery, sleep, workout, and body measurement data via the WHOOP API. You can disconnect at any time from the app settings.

We only access the specific data types you authorize. We do not access data beyond what is needed for fitness tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Your data may be shared only in these cases:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app (e.g., our database host), under strict data processing agreements.
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • With other users, only for features you explicitly opt into (e.g., public leaderboards, activity feed posts).

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is protected by authentication and row-level security policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can:

  • Disconnect integrations: Remove any connected service from the app settings at any time. This stops future data syncing from that service.
  • Delete your account: You can delete your account from the app settings. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Solo Fitness is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
