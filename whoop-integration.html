Solo Fitness - How WHOOP data is integrated into the app experience

Overview

Solo Fitness is a comprehensive fitness tracking app that aggregates data from multiple sources. The WHOOP integration brings recovery, strain, sleep, and workout data directly into the user's fitness dashboard, providing a unified view of their health metrics.

Users connect their WHOOP account via OAuth 2.0 from the Settings screen. Once connected, data syncs automatically every 30 minutes.

Connection Flow

1

Settings > Apps & Devices

User navigates to Settings and finds WHOOP listed under "Direct Connections" with a toggle to connect.

Disconnect Flow

Users can disconnect WHOOP at any time from Settings > Apps & Devices by toggling off the WHOOP connection. A confirmation dialog ensures no accidental disconnects. After disconnecting, no further data is fetched from WHOOP. Previously synced data remains in the app.
